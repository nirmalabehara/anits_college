Admission Procedure

Admissions to the B.Tech programs are conducted through the Engineering, Agriculture and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test (EAPCET) for Category-A seats. For Category-B (Management/NRI) seats, the institute conducts its own admission process. Detailed information is available on the official website.

Departments

Computer Science & Engineering

Chemical Engineering

The Department of Chemical Engineering specializes in designing processes and systems for the production of chemicals, fuel, pharmaceuticals, and other products. The curriculum includes core principles of chemistry, biology, and environmental engineering.

CSE (AI & ML)

CSE (Data Science)

Mechanical Engineering

The Department of Mechanical Engineering provides students with knowledge in designing, analyzing, and manufacturing mechanical systems, with applications in robotics, thermodynamics, and material science.

Civil Engineering

Information Technology
